Who We Are

Building Bridges Between Talent & Opportunity

Ardent Recruiters is a US-based IT staffing firm with its roots firmly in Gujarat, India. We specialize in connecting skilled IT professionals — especially those on OPT/CPT and H1B visas — with top American technology companies.

Our team doesn't just match resumes to job descriptions. We coach, train, and advocate for every candidate from the first consultation through their first day of work — and beyond.
