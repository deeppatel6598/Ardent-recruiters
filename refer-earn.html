FAQ

Referral Questions

How do I track my referrals?
Once you register as a referrer, you'll receive a personal dashboard to track your referrals, their status, and your pending and paid rewards in real time.
When do I get paid?
Rewards are processed within 30 days of successful placement confirmation. You can receive payment via bank transfer or other available methods.
Is there a limit on how many people I can refer?
No limit! You can refer as many people as you like. The more successful referrals you make, the higher your plan tier and the larger your reward per placement.
What counts as a "successful placement"?
A successful placement is when your referred candidate enrolls with Ardent Recruiters and is placed in a full-time IT position at one of our employer partner companies.
