FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what candidates and employers ask us most.

Do you work with OPT and CPT students?
Yes! We specialize in placing OPT and CPT students in US-based IT roles. Our team has deep experience navigating the authorization requirements and connecting international graduates with employers who sponsor work visas.
Can you help with H1B visa sponsorship?
We work with a network of H1B-friendly employers and can help position you for sponsorship opportunities. While we don't file petitions directly, we connect you with employers who do and guide you through the process.
What types of IT jobs do you place candidates in?
We place candidates across the full spectrum of IT roles: software developers, data scientists, QA engineers, business analysts, project managers, cloud architects, cybersecurity specialists, and more — across 50+ industries.
Is there a fee for job seekers?
Our job placement consultation is completely free for candidates. We earn our fee from employers upon a successful hire, so there's never any cost to job seekers for our core placement services.
How long does the placement process take?
Our average placement time is 45 days from initial consultation to offer letter. This varies based on your profile, target roles, and market conditions — but our dedicated team works hard to move as quickly as possible.
Do you offer remote positions?
Yes. A significant portion of our employer partners offer remote or hybrid roles. We'll match you with opportunities that fit your preferred work arrangement.
What does your IT training program cover?
Our IT training covers role-based skills (Java, Python, data engineering, testing, cloud, etc.), resume and LinkedIn optimization, interview preparation with mock sessions, and practical project work to build your portfolio.
How does background verification work for employers?
We handle employment verification, education verification, criminal background checks, and reference verification. We've completed 500K+ checks at 99.9% accuracy. Turnaround time is typically 3–5 business days.
Do you have offices in India?
Yes — we have three India offices in Visnagar, Ahmedabad, and Vadodara (all in Gujarat). Our India team handles candidate sourcing, training, and initial screening, while our Florida HQ manages US employer relationships and placements.
How does the Refer & Earn program work?
Simply refer a friend to Ardent Recruiters, they enroll in our program, and when they're placed, you earn a reward — ranging from $200 (Starter) to $400 (Elite). Over 3,750 referrers have collectively earned $2.4M+ through our program.
